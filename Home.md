## Things We Recommend
These resources are things we have first-hand experience of and can testify to the quality of.

If you've had a good experience with a resource, edit this page and increment the üëç. Feel free to move stuff from the things-we-found section up to here if you've personally had a good experience with it. If you're uncertain, come discuss it in #wiki first.

### Written Introductions
 * 1üëç [Spinning Up](https://spinningup.openai.com/) is somewhere between an extended tutorial and a short course, with a practical bent. 
 * 1üëç [Sutton & Barto's *Introduction to Reinforcement Learning*](https://bit.ly/3oiyUEw) is the standard intro textbook.

### Lecture Videos
 * 1üëç [Introduction to Reinforcement Learning with David Silver](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLqYmG7hTraZBiG_XpjnPrSNw-1XQaM_gB), with [slides](https://www.davidsilver.uk/teaching/)

### Miscellaneous
